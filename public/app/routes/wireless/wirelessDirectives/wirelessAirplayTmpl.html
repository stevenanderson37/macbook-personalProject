AirPlay

Show off your photos, video,
and more on your HDTV.

Sharing a presentation or a video clip from your computer to a TV shouldn’t require any hassle or complicated setup. With AirPlay, you can wirelessly stream what’s on your MacBook to your HDTV and speakers using Apple TV.3 Or mirror exactly what’s on your display to the big screen.4 Guests in your home or office can even use AirPlay to stream from their devices without having to connect to your Wi‑Fi network.5 Vacation photos, home movies, presentations — AirPlay and Apple TV put it all on your TV.
